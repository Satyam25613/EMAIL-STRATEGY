About This Architecture Document

This document serves as the central strategic blueprint and actionable repository for the KaraX.ai Post-Signup Email Engine.

Why does this exist? To map exactly how KaraX communicates with its users from the moment they sign up to deep into their paid retention phase. This strategy uses precise behavioral triggers (like 30-minute inactivity or 5th task completion) to deliver clean, highly-targeted, human-sounding emails. By bypassing traditional "marketing blasts," we establish an invisible, high-value ecosystem orchestrator.

Below, you will find 6 defined logic modules. Each module explains when to trigger the communication, why it is necessary from a behavioral standpoint, and provides a direct link to preview the production-verified, minimalist HTML template.

Sequence 01

The Activation Push

Trigger Condition
Exactly 30 minutes post-signup, if zero workloads have been executed.
Strategic Objective
Bypass UI friction. Give them a direct link to initiate their first automation output.

Friction Reduction & The Zeigarnik Effect

Users remember incomplete loops (signing up but doing nothing). Instead of sending a heavy "Welcome Tutorial", we lower the barrier to zero. We ask for one single problem to solve in 60 seconds.
